Senior Project

A hybrid animation video trailer created in collaboration with my colleagues for my senior project. For this project, I was a 3D modeler, 3D animator, and color designer.

My work on this project is shown in the slideshow below.

My models and animations shown in the first four slides were all created in Autodesk Maya. I also controlled all the lighting for every scene in Autodesk Maya, and rendered all colors with the Toon shade material to give a cartoon appearance. I also worked across different animation software; my colleagues created their models (images after the fourth slide) and animations in 3D Studio Max, which I exported as a compatible file into Maya in order to use the Toon Shade material and to maintain a cohesive final product. The still images in the slideshow were all rendered at a low quality as color examples to save time when working on the project.
