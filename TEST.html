Hello, my name is Adair.

I love all things web. My driving passion is to succeed in making every web project I'm faced with function as beautifully as it looks. Design and user experience are both quite inspiring to me, and I am constantly working towards expanding my knowledge on them.

I'm also a big fan of video games, music, and film. Any form of art I have been met with has always been a big part of my life, web development included!
