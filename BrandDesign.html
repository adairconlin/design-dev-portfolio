Brand Design

"Kiwi Imports" is a personal project for a concept brand that I created in order to showcase my ability to design a cohesive brand identity. The concept for this brand was an environmental friendly modern clothing brand for teens and young adults.

"knotted co." is a concept brand I created in order to showcase my ability to design a cohesive brand identiy. This concept brand is based on that idea of a music-sharing app where you can stream your favorite music from any music listening app to listen with your friends simultaneously. Users also have the ability to record and upload their own musical content to share with others. I wanted a relaxing, warm, and inviting tone to this brand identity to reflect a laid back acoustic musician-vibe.

"NotYourWa1fu" is a brand name created by the Twitch streamer NotYourWa1fu who I worked with to create a cohesive brand identity piece. Everything I designed is based on her pre-existing Twitch channel and aesthetics.
