About Me

Hello! I'm Adair Conlin and I am an animation graduate located in Charlotte, North Carolina. I am passionate for any creative project, whether it be digital or traditional. Colors especially inspire me the most, and I could obsess over fonts with someone for hours. I aim to progress my understanding in the design field as I continue my adventure in this career path.

I have a growing drive for web design and UI/UX development. My experience in this pursuit has been using Adobe Ilustrator and Adobe XD to create assets and to flesh out overall design ideas. I have an understanding of HTML, CSS, Bootstrap 4, and some JavaScript. A lot of my time has centered around following the latest trends in web design, as well as constantly learning more about web development to advance my skills. My secondary interest is brand design, as it thrills me to bring an idea of a company to life, or advance a company's existing brand, through well thought-out and purposeful designs, colors, and assets.

Outside of my passion for art I enjoy playing guitar, video games, and watching movies; I especially love horror movies. I also have a love for nature and animals, which I enjoy through outdoor activities and spending most of my free time at home playing with my ferrets.
