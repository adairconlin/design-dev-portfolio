Brand Design:

Kiwi Imports NotYourWa1fu The_Apprent1ce

"Kiwi Imports" is a personal concept project for a brand that I created in order to showcase my ability to design a cohesive brand identity. The concept for this brand was an environment-friendly modern clothing brand for teens and young adults.

I used Adobe Illustrator to design the logos, markers, and brand elements, and to create a color palette as well as the overall direction board that is shown below.

I used Adobe Photoshop CC to create mockups of potential merchandise for the company as well as business cards. I also designed a website for Kiwi Imports in Adobe XD, then created a visual of it displayed on a laptop in Adobe Photoshop CC.

All photos used for mockups were taken from Unsplash.com.


"NotYourWa1fu" is a brand name created by the Twitch streamer NotYourWa1fu who I worked with to create a cohesive brand identity piece. Everything I designed is based on her pre-existing Twitch channel and aesthetics.

I used Adobe Illustrator to design the logos, markers, and brand elements, and to create a color palette as well as the overall direction board that is shown below.

The floral brand elements used are designed by pch.vector/Freepik.
