About Me

Hello! I'm Adair Conlin and I am an animation graduate located in Charlotte, North Carolina. I am passionate for any creative project, whether it be digital or traditional art. Colors especially inspire me the most, and I could obsess over fonts with someone for hours. I aim to progress my understanding in the design field as I continue my adventure in this career path.

I have experience with traditional art, digital art, 2D animation, 3D modeling, 3D animation. My time spent with traditional art focused on drawing portraits, typography, and sketches for graphic design projects. Most of what is on my website shares my work in digital art, including digital portraits and graphic design. I also design websites using Adobe Ilustrator and Adobe XD, as well as develop websites with my knowledge of html, css, Bootstrap 4, and javascript. Some miscellaneous art hobbies I enjoy include painting and pyrography.

Outside of my passion for art I enjoy playing guitar, video games, and watching movies; I especially love horror movies. I also have a love for nature and animals, which I enjoy through outdoor activities and spending most of my free time at home playing with my ferrets.
